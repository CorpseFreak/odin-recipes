Dads Brandy Ice

Some dudes dad made carton after carton of this spiked thick, creamy malted at Christmas time when I was growing up. He would give it to friends. He was very popular.

  • 6 fluid ounces brandy
  • 1 fluid ounce triple sec
  • 1 fluid ounce creme de cacao
  • 1 fluid ounce honey- and herb-flavored whiskey liqueur (such as Drambuie®)
  • ½ gallon vanilla ice cream
  1. Mix brandy, triple sec, creme de cacao, and whiskey liqueur in a glass measuring cup.
  2. Put half the vanilla ice cream and half the brandy mixture in a blender. Blend until smooth. Add remaining ice cream and brandy mixture to blender. Blend again until smooth. Store in freezer in the ice cream container.
